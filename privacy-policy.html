Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

MrbroSL Posts Viewer is a mobile application that displays public posts from the MrbroSL Facebook page. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

Our app collects and displays:

  • Public Facebook Posts: Content that is publicly available on the MrbroSL Facebook page
  • Page Information: Public page details like follower count and description
  • Engagement Metrics: Public like, comment, and share counts

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Display posts from the MrbroSL Facebook page
  • Show engagement metrics and page statistics
  • Provide a user-friendly interface for viewing content
  • Auto-update posts every 5 minutes

3. Data Storage

Important: We do NOT store any personal information or post content on our servers. All data is:

  • Retrieved directly from Facebook's public API
  • Displayed temporarily in the app
  • Not saved or cached permanently

4. Facebook Integration

Our app integrates with Facebook through:

  • Facebook Graph API for public page data
  • Required permissions for reading public page content
  • No access to private user information

5. Third-Party Services

We use these third-party services:

  • Facebook Graph API: For retrieving public page posts
  • Expo/React Native: For app development framework
  • Picsum Photos: For placeholder images (when Facebook images fail)

6. User Rights

As a user of our app, you have the right to:

  • View public content from the MrbroSL page
  • Refresh content manually or automatically
  • Change page names to view different Facebook pages
  • Uninstall the app at any time

7. Data Security

We implement security measures to:

  • Protect API credentials and access tokens
  • Ensure secure communication with Facebook servers
  • Maintain app integrity and user privacy

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Information

Questions about this Privacy Policy?

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us through the app or email us.

11. Legal Basis

This privacy policy is based on:

  • Facebook Platform Policy requirements
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • Best practices for mobile app privacy
Note: This app only displays publicly available information from Facebook pages. We do not collect, store, or process any personal user data.

This privacy policy is effective as of January 2025 and will remain in effect except with respect to any changes in its provisions in the future.
