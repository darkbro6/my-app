Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: January 2025

MrbroSL Posts Viewer is committed to protecting your privacy and providing clear instructions on how to request data deletion in compliance with data protection regulations.

1. What Data We Collect

Our app collects and displays:

  • Public Facebook Posts: Content that is publicly available on the MrbroSL Facebook page
  • Page Information: Public page details like follower count and description
  • Engagement Metrics: Public like, comment, and share counts
Important: We do NOT collect, store, or process any personal user data on our servers.

2. Data Storage Policy

Our app operates on a no-storage basis:

  • All data is retrieved directly from Facebook's public API
  • No personal information is stored on our servers
  • No user data is cached or saved permanently
  • Data is only displayed temporarily in the app

3. How to Request Data Deletion

Since we don't store personal data, here are your options:

Option 1: Uninstall the App

Immediate Action:

  • Remove the app from your device
  • This stops all data collection immediately
  • No personal data remains in our system

Option 2: Contact Us

For Additional Requests:

  • Email us with your deletion request
  • We'll confirm no data exists in our systems
  • We'll remove any app preferences if stored locally

Option 3: Facebook Data Deletion

For Facebook-Related Data:

  • Go to Facebook Settings â†’ Your Facebook Information
  • Request deletion of data shared with our app
  • This removes any permissions granted to our app

4. What Happens When You Request Deletion

When you request data deletion:

  • Immediate: App stops collecting any new data
  • Within 24 hours: We confirm no data exists in our systems
  • Within 30 days: We provide written confirmation of deletion
  • Ongoing: We maintain records of deletion requests for compliance

5. Verification Process

To verify your deletion request:

  • Provide your email address or app identifier
  • We'll search our systems for any stored data
  • We'll confirm what data (if any) was found and deleted
  • We'll provide a certificate of deletion

6. Data Retention After Deletion

After processing your deletion request:

  • No personal data remains in our systems
  • Deletion request records are kept for legal compliance (7 years)
  • App analytics may retain anonymous, aggregated data
  • No individual user data is retained

This data deletion policy is based on:
